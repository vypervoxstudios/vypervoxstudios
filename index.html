# Privacy Policy **Effective Date:** July 7, 2026 Welcome to **VyperVox Studio**. At VyperVox Studio, we respect your privacy. Our games are designed to provide an enjoyable experience while collecting as little information as possible. ## Information We Collect Our games **do not collect, store, or share any personal information** such as your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personally identifiable information. We do not require users to create an account or provide personal details to play our games. ## Advertisements Our games may display advertisements provided by trusted third-party advertising partners (such as Google AdMob). These advertising services may automatically collect certain non-personal information, including device information, advertising identifiers, IP address, and general usage data, to deliver relevant ads, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud. This information is collected directly by the advertising provider according to their own privacy policies. ## Children's Privacy Our games are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe any personal information has been collected unintentionally, please contact us so we can address the issue promptly. ## Third-Party Services Our games may use third-party services, including advertising providers, that have their own privacy policies. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of those third-party services to understand how their information is handled. ## Data Security Since we do not collect or store personal information, we do not maintain databases containing user personal data. We are committed to using trusted services and reasonable security practices to help ensure a safe gaming experience. ## Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our games after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. ## Contact Us If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: **VyperVox Studio** **Email:** [support@vypervoxstudio.com](mailto:support@vypervoxstudio.com)
